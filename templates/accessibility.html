Inclusion

Accessibility

Mental health support should be available to everyone. We are committed to building an inclusive platform that works for all people, regardless of ability.

♿

WCAG 2.1 Level AA Compliant

Targeting full compliance with international accessibility standards

👁️
Visual Accessibility

High contrast mode, adjustable font sizes from 14px to 24px, color-blind-safe palette, and support for screen magnifiers.

⌨️
Keyboard Navigation

Fully navigable via keyboard alone. Logical tab order, visible focus indicators, and comprehensive keyboard shortcuts throughout.

🔊
Screen Reader Support

Tested with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver. Semantic HTML5, ARIA labels, live regions for dynamic content updates.

🎯
Motor Accessibility

Large click/tap targets (minimum 44×44px), reduced motion mode, voice input compatibility, and no time-limited interactions.

🧠
Cognitive Accessibility

Plain language, consistent navigation, clear error messages, no flashing content, and distraction-free reading mode.

📱
Mobile Accessibility

Full iOS VoiceOver and Android TalkBack support. Touch-friendly design with gesture alternatives for all interactions.

Known Limitations

We are continuously improving. Currently known limitations include:

  • Some older charts in analytics may have limited screen reader descriptions — we are adding detailed alt text.
  • Video content does not yet have closed captions — captions are in development for Q2 2025.
  • PDF exports may not be fully accessible — we are working on tagged PDF output.

Browser & Assistive Technology Support

We test with the following combinations:

  • NVDA + Firefox (Windows)
  • JAWS + Chrome (Windows)
  • VoiceOver + Safari (macOS and iOS)
  • TalkBack + Chrome (Android)
