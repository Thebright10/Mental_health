🤝 Community Guidelines

💚

Lead with Empathy

Speak from experience, not advice. Use "I" statements and listen before responding.

🔒

Respect Anonymity

What's shared here stays here. Never share others' stories outside the community.

🚫

No Harmful Content

Do not share detailed descriptions of self-harm methods, substances, or crisis-triggering content.

🌱

Growth Mindset

Celebrate all progress, no matter how small. Healing is nonlinear and every step counts.
