Practices
🧘
Mindfulness Meditation
10–20 min daily

Anchor attention to the present by focusing on breath, body sensations, or sounds. Reduces anxiety and improves focus within weeks.

🌊
Body Scan
15–30 min

Progressively relax each part of the body from toes to crown. Deeply effective for releasing tension and improving sleep quality.

💜
Loving-Kindness (Metta)
10–15 min

Cultivate compassion for yourself and others. Clinically shown to reduce depression, loneliness, and interpersonal conflict.

🌬️
Pranayama Breathing
5–10 min

Ancient yogic breath control including 4-7-8, alternate nostril, and box breathing. Instantly modulates your nervous system state.

🌹
NSDR / Yoga Nidra
20 min

Non-Sleep Deep Rest protocol used by neuroscientists. Restores mental energy equivalent to hours of sleep in just 20 minutes.

🚶
Walking Meditation
10–30 min

Mindful walking combines movement with presence. Ideal for those who struggle with seated practice or need mood improvement.
